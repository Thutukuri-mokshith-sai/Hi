Get Started with Your AYUSH Startup Registration

Welcome to the AYUSH Startup Registration Portal! We're here to guide you through the process of registering your AYUSH startup. Follow the steps below to get started:

Step 1: Sign Up or Log In

Create a new account if you're a first-time user, or log in if you already have an account.

Step 2: Fill Out the Application

Complete the registration form with accurate details about your startup. Make sure to review the information before submission.

Step 3: Upload Necessary Documents

Upload all the required documents to ensure compliance with AYUSH regulations. Double-check the document list to avoid any delays.

Step 4: Track Your Application Status

After submission, you can track the status of your application in real-time. We'll notify you as soon as your application is reviewed.

Step 5: Receive Your Approval

Once your application is approved, you'll receive your registration certificate. Congratulations on taking your first step toward success!
