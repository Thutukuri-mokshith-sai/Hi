Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I register a startup?

To register a startup, you need to visit the Application Submission page and fill out the required forms with your startup details.

What documents are required for registration?

You will need to upload documents such as your Aadhar card, proof of business address, and any relevant AYUSH certifications. Visit the Document Upload page for more details.

How can I track my application status?

You can track the status of your application in real-time by visiting the Status Tracking page.

Who can I contact for support?

For support, you can use the contact form below or check our Support Resources section for more information.
